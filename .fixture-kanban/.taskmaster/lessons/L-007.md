Layered elevation in this codebase uses surface tokens (`--s1` < `--s2` < `--s3` < `--s4`) — never `box-shadow`. Shadows look dated and cheap; surface stepping reads as honest depth. Same applies to focus/hover states: change color/border/background, not transform.
