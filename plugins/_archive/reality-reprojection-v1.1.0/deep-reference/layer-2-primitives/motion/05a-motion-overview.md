## Preamble

Typography gives the system its voice. Color gives it atmosphere. Spacing gives it structure. Motion gives it **time** — the dimension in which the system becomes alive, reactive, and present.

Motion is how the system responds to the passage of time and the presence of the user. Without motion, the system is a photograph. With it, the system *inhabits* time.

This document defines the **Clockwork** — the mechanism by which Reality Reprojection relates to time — and the **Collection** of motion characters available within it. Specific easing curves, duration values, and implementation patterns are Layer 3 concerns. What Layer 2 establishes is what the system *believes* about motion.

---

## A Cross-Motion Trait — Motion is Clockwork

Across every motion decision in Reality Reprojection, the governing instinct is that motion is **engineered precision you stop to admire**. Not physics simulation. Not decorative flourish. Motion as a mechanism — visible or felt — where every moving part has a purpose, and the quality of the mechanism itself communicates care.

A clockwork is not just mechanical. It is mechanical *and beautiful*. The jeweled bearing is also the detail that catches light. This dual nature — function that is simultaneously craft — is the temporal expression of Craftsmanship as Respect.

Motion is Clockwork also means: **every motion has a mechanism behind it**, even if hidden. A transition that feels right because the easing was tuned with intention. A reactive response that feels natural because the relationship between input and output was calibrated.

The machine has a heartbeat. Motion is where you hear it.
