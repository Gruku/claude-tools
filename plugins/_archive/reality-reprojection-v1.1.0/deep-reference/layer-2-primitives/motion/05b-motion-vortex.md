## The Time Vortex

*The raw temporal substrate. The passage of time, the presence of the user, and the conditions that make motion possible.*

### The Premise

Motion exists because the user is present. The user's presence is the winding of the mainspring. Without it, the clockwork is still.

The Time Vortex is the continuous flow of time and interaction that creates the conditions for all motion. It is not a motion character itself — it is the medium motion moves through. The river, not the boat.

### Reactivity Lives Here

The user's interaction creates disturbances in the Vortex:
- A **hover** is a ripple — system registering proximity, responding with subtle shift
- A **click** is a puncture — committed action triggers mechanical response
- A **scroll** is navigation through the vortex — viewport lens moving across canvas
- A **drag** is direct manipulation — user's hand on mechanism, turning the gear

Reactivity is not a feature bolted on. It is the fundamental premise. The clockwork responds to the person winding it.

The intensity of reactivity follows the dials. A playful project responds richly to mouse movement. A dense data dashboard responds more sparingly. The Vortex is always flowing. How much the system reveals its awareness is a dial position.

### What the Vortex Is Not

The Vortex is not chaos. It is not a license for constant ambient motion — elements drifting, pulsing, orbiting without purpose. The clockwork is wound by the user. When the user is still, the system can be still.

Ambient motion — slowly rotating element, breathing pulse, background animation — must earn its place. It is the system's idle tick, not its restless fidgeting. If nothing is happening, the system is allowed to wait.
