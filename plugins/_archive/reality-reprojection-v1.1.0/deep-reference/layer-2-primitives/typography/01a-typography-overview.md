## Preamble

Typography is the most load-bearing element in Reality Reprojection. Before color, before layout, before motion — type is where the identity becomes visible. The Monumental↔Intimate tension, the dramatic confidence, the technical precision, the warmth underneath — type carries all of it.

This document does not prescribe specific typefaces. It defines **voices** — the distinct speaking modes the system needs — their jobs, their personalities, their ranges, and their rules. Specific fonts are cast into these roles; the roles themselves are durable. A typeface can be replaced. The voice it serves cannot.

---

## The Ensemble

Reality Reprojection's type system is built on **three instruments**, each with expressive range. Not five fonts doing five jobs — three voices that stretch.

The analogy is a speaker at a lectern. One person who can command a room, explain something technical, carry a long narrative, and say something small that makes you lean in. Different modes. Same speaker.

---

## The Three Voices (Quick Reference)

| Voice | Job | Character | Range |
|-------|-----|-----------|-------|
| **Declaration** | Headlines, identity, monumental moments | Heavy, present, authoritative, crafted | Display sizes, statement-making |
| **Narrator** | Reading, explaining, structure | Calm, humanist, generous readability | Body text, labels, interface |
| **Technical** | Code, data, precision | Monospace, unambiguous, metrically precise | Small-to-medium sizes, constrained contexts |

Each voice is designed to handle a distinct category of content and carries its own philosophy about how type should behave in that context. Together, they create a system with both constraint and range.

## Connection to Layer 1

The three voices embody Layer 1's core belief: **Constraints generate clarity**. The system doesn't use five fonts doing five jobs. It uses three voices that stretch across a range of expression. Each voice earns its place by doing a job no other voice can.

The tension between voices (Declaration's boldness vs. Narrator's calm, Narrator's organic curves vs. Technical's grid) carries the **Monumental ↔ Intimate** and **Technical ↔ Human** tensions from Layer 1 directly into typography.
