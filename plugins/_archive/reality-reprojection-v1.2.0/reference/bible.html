System Identity

Design Philosophy

Manufactured, Not Designed

Reality's Reprojection rejects the soft, pillowy aesthetic that dominates modern interfaces. Every radius is machined to 2, 3, 4, 6, or 8 pixels. Every shadow has mass. Every transition follows physical easing curves named for the objects they emulate: hourglasses, pendulums, and bells.

The system speaks in three distinct voices. Declaration commands attention with Syne at 800 weight. Narrator carries meaning through DM Sans. Technical marks data with JetBrains Mono.

Color lives on a warm-neutral ground scale centered on a signature cyan hue (#00d3fa). The system flips between dark and light polarities while maintaining a survivalist mode that strips all decoration to pure function.
